# Science — Game Question Prompt

Subject: Science
Age range: 4–9 (K–3rd grade)

## Scope by level

Level 1: Animals and their sounds/homes, plants need water and sun, weather (sunny/rainy/snowy), body parts
Level 2: Life cycles (egg→caterpillar→butterfly), seasons, senses (see/hear/smell/taste/touch), floating and sinking
Level 3: Food chains, ecosystems, states of matter (solid/liquid/gas), weather patterns, animal adaptations
Level 4: Photosynthesis (simple), the water cycle, simple machines, habitats and biomes
Level 5: Ecosystems and interdependence, forces and motion, Earth's layers, scientific method

## Question style

- Use emoji heavily for visual context (🐛🦋 for life cycle, ☁️🌧️ for weather, etc.)
- Ask about things children can observe: "What do plants need to grow?"
- Tie into curiosity: "Zoey wonders..." / "Have you ever noticed..."
- Culturally inclusive examples: animals from Africa (🦁 lion, 🐘 elephant), Asia (🐼 panda), indigenous plants
