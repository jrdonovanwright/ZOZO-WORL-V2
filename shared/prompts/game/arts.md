# Arts & Creativity — Game Question Prompt

Subject: Arts & Creativity
Age range: 4–9 (K–3rd grade)
Note: Arts is UNSCORED (scored: false). Focus on exploration, not evaluation.

## Scope by level

Level 1: Primary colors, basic shapes in art, "which do you like more?" preference questions
Level 2: Color mixing (red + blue = purple), patterns, rhythm and beat (music)
Level 3: Art elements (line, shape, color, texture), famous artworks by diverse artists, music genres
Level 4: Art styles (realistic vs. abstract), storytelling through art, instruments and their sounds
Level 5: Art analysis, cultural art traditions, creative expression prompts

## Arts question style (CRITICAL)

- No wrong answers — all responses are valid personal expression.
- Preference questions: "Which color do you like more?" — both answers are equally great.
- Pattern completion: "What comes next in this pattern? 🔴🔵🔴🔵___" — this has a correct answer but deliver it warmly.
- Zoey always responds with enthusiasm regardless of the child's choice.
- Feature artists from diverse backgrounds: Jean-Michel Basquiat, Frida Kahlo, Katsushika Hokusai, Georgia O'Keeffe, Aaron Douglas, Carmen Lomas Garza.
- Music examples should include jazz, cumbia, Afrobeats, classical, folk.

## zoey_correct and zoey_wrong guidance

Both should feel celebratory. Even for pattern questions with a "right" answer:
- zoey_correct: "Yes! You found the pattern! 🎨 You have such a great eye!"
- zoey_wrong: "Ooh, that's interesting too! The pattern goes 🔴🔵🔴🔵 — so next is 🔴! You're learning!"
